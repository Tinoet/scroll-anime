Give Yourself A Break

HI!!!

I used to always feel this Pressure to have the most

Productive
Non-Stop
Mind Blowing Action Packed day

And when I couldn't reach that Impossibly Unattainable Goal

I would get really Upset with Myself

But then one day I Decided that

My Mental Health Means More to me than my Productivity

I am not A Machine and I dont want to Be

If this is my one little life on this floaty circle Rock

Then I want to spend it Honoring my Body and Feelings

it could mean running a marathon or getting out of bed

which in my opinion are Pretty tight Schedules

Anyway, this is just a Reminder to give yourself a Break

You have so many Days to do so many Things

whatever you are supposed to get Done is gonna get Done

I promise
and

I LoveYou so Much.
